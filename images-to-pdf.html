Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between A4 and Letter?

A4 (210×297mm) is the international standard used in most countries. Letter (8.5×11 inches) is the US standard. Choose based on where your document will be printed.

What does "Fit to Image" do?

Instead of fixed page sizes, each page is sized exactly to fit its image dimensions. Great for photo books or when you want to preserve original aspect ratios.

How large can the PDF be?

Since processing happens in your browser, size depends on your device's memory. For best results, keep total image size under 100MB. Use lower quality setting for smaller PDFs.
