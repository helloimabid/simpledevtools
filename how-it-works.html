How SimpleDevTools Works

Everything runs locally. Your data never leaves your browser.

1. Paste your data

Input your code, image, or text directly into the tool's interface.

2. Local Processing

JavaScript running in your browser processes the input instantly.

3. Instant Result

Get your formatted or converted output immediately. No network calls.

Why Client-Side?

Security and speed by design.

No Server Uploads

Your sensitive data (JSON, Base64 strings, or images) is never sent to our servers. It stays in your RAM.

No User Tracking

We don't use analytics scripts that record your inputs or behavior. We respect your privacy.

Zero Persistence

Once you close the tab, your data is gone. We don't store anything in databases or local storage.

Blazing Fast

Since there's no network latency, tools react instantly. Works offline once the page is loaded.

Privacy Policy Summary

SimpleDevTools is open regarding its operations. We only use local storage for your theme preference (Dark/Light mode). No cookies for tracking purposes are set.
