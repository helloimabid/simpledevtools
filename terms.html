Privacy Policy

We respect your data.

Our philosophy is "Client-Side First". This means your code, JSON, and text inputs generally never leave your browser.

Last updated: October 24, 2023

Data Collection & Processing

When you use tools on SimpleDevTools (such as JSON Formatters, SQL Converters, or Diff Checkers), the processing is performed locally within your browser using JavaScript.

We do not send your code snippets to our servers.

We do not store, view, or analyze the content you paste into our tools. Once you close the tab, that data is gone.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use Local Storage to save your preferences, such as Dark Mode settings. This data is strictly stored on your device and is not synchronized with us.

However, we use third-party services that may place cookies on your browser:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how users navigate our site (anonymized data).
  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

GDPR & CCPA Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since we do not collect personal accounts or code data, our ability to identify you is limited to standard web logs.

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
